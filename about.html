I got into UX/UI because of my interests in visual design, accessibility, interaction design and the desire to make a strong social impact. My career up to this point has had many twists and turns, but I've found ways to leverage all my past experiences on my journey to grow as a UX/UI Designer.

My story so far...

While studying Cartooning & Illustration at The School of Visual Arts, I learned a lot about the general creative process and leveraged my visual thinking skills. As a teacher for young adults with developmental disabilities, I saw the impact I had on their lives through the use of accessible technologies. From my time as a makeup artist, I developed my ability to translate an individual's wants into visually appealing results. Several years spent playing roller derby taught me about the value of dedication, determination, and teamwork.

In my free time, you can find me sitting in a cozy chair with a cup of tea working on my sewing or cross-stitching. I enjoy drawing and attending to my numerous house plants.
