Hi, I'm Morgan

I'm a Product Designer with a strong interest in visual design, accessibility, interaction design, and the desire to make a strong social impact.

My design approach

My approach to design is deeply collaborative, and I thrive in environments where I can work cross-functionally with Software Engineers, Product Managers, and fellow Designers. I am passionate about utilizing design thinking to iterate on complex problems and transform ideas into user-centric solutions.

Other interests

In addition to my work as a designer, I lead an adventurous life with my energetic one-year-old rescue pup. Together, we often take long walks in the woods and explore the world around us.

I am deeply involved in the roller derby community, not just as a competitor but also as an officiant. This role allows me to contribute to the community's spirit and ensure fairness in competition.

My participation in roller derby has honed my ability to make quick decisions and work effectively under pressure, skills that are invaluable in the fast-paced field of product design.
